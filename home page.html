Welcome to People's Fellowship

Here at People's Fellowship, we love people - all people. Our goal is to be hope for the hopeless, healing for the hurting, and salvation through Jesus Christ to those who are lost. We offer Bible-based preaching and worship, and a time of prayer for people's needs during each service. Although we are an Assemblies of God church, our church family has a variety of backgrounds. We offer a friendly, welcoming atmosphere from the moment you first walk in, up until you leave. We hope to see you in one of our gatherings soon. God bless you!

Ministries we offer: Wednesday nights, 6:30pm

    Wednesday Night Bible Study:

    We take a deep dive into God's word. Come join us as we explore more about God's plans and purposes for our lives.

    Youth Ministry:

    Our teens and youth not only socialize and have fun, but they are also equipped and challenged by God's word every Wednesday night. Don't miss out!

    Overcomers:

    We have a heart of compassion for those who have stuggled or are struggling with addiction. We provide a safe space where we minister to those with these struggles.
