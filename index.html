Welcome to People's Fellowship

Here at People's Fellowship, we love people - all people. Our goal is to be hope for the hopeless, healing for the hurting, and salvation through Jesus Christ to those who are lost. We offer Bible-based preaching and worship, and a time of prayer for people's needs during each service. Although we are an Assemblies of God church, our church family has a variety of backgrounds. We offer a friendly, welcoming atmosphere from the moment you first walk in, up until you leave. We hope to see you in one of our gatherings:

Sunday Morning @10:30am Wednesday Nights @6:30pm Thursday Morning Prayer @11:30am

God Bless You!

Our Ministries:

Wednesday Night Bible Study:
Join us as we take a deep dive into the word of God, exploring and discussing various Biblical topics.
Wednesday Night Youth:
Our teens also gather for a time of fellowship and bible study. Come join us!
Overcomers:
We have a heart of compassion for those who are struggling with addiction or any other life crisis. We provide a safe space where we minister to those with these struggles.
